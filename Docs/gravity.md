## Overview
Gravity is a tactical, simultaneous-turn sci-fi strategy game. Captains plan orders in parallel, lock the turn, and the engine resolves every action at once. The digital client mirrors the current tabletop rules for full parity.

## Modes
- **Single-player**: Submit and resolve instantly for fast iteration.
- **Online multiplayer**: Concurrent planning; the turn resolves when all captains lock in.
- **Local/hotseat**: Share a device, plan in turns, resolve together.

## Core Loop
1) Plan actions for your crew and ship systems.
2) Lock orders; once everyone is ready, resolve the turn simultaneously.
3) Survive hazards, score missions, and race to escape the gravity well.

## What Makes Gravity Different
- **True simultaneous resolution**: No initiative stack—outcomes are determined after all orders lock.
- **Ship systems & power routing**: Manage six ship sections, conduits, shields, and power transfers with overload risk.
- **Crew roles & captains**: Unique captains, officers, and specialists change how you restore power, maneuver, repair, revive, scan, acquire, and attack.
- **Deterministic outcomes**: Transparent rules (mission VP, turn order, damage, revive thresholds) match the current rules engine.
- **Orbiting board**: Eight rotating rings, environmental hazards, and object movement shape positioning.
- **High-stakes actions**: Maneuver with acceleration bonuses, targeted attacks, repairs with multipliers, revives with thresholds, scans/acquires for resources, launches/retaliates, assemble/integrate upgrades.
- **Escape race**: Victory points reward escape timing, missions, ship state, crew, and stored resources.

## Current Feature Parity (Digital ↔ Tabletop)
- Turn structure matches the published order: Event → Restore Power → routing → Revive → Repair → Maneuver → Scan → Acquire → Attack → Launch → Retaliate → Assemble → Integrate → Auto-generate → Environment/Hazard damage → Orbit/object movement → Escape/wreck checks.
- Action economy: 1 action per active crew; explicit extra-action sources only (Cybernetics, Temporal Shift). Maneuver limited to 1 per turn.
- Systems: Power generation bonuses by section/role, conduit overload limits, life support as a power pool, shields vs. bypassing damage, environment and hazard damage applied post-resolution.
- Roles & upgrades: Captain/officer/basic crew bonuses for power, repair, revive, maneuver; Droid Station, Nano-Bots, Plasma/Ion/Temporal upgrades; explorer kit and spare parts behaviors.
- Victory: VP from escape order, missions (with multipliers), ship state, crew, and stored resources; tie-breakers implemented.

## Roster & Content
- **Ships**: Single ship per player with Bridge, Engineering, Drives, Med-Lab, Sci-Lab, Defense.
- **Crew**: 1 Captain, 2 Officers, 5 Basic crew, plus Android; roles confer action bonuses.
- **Cards/Decks**: Mission, Event, Upgrade decks aligned with current rule values.

## Monetization
- **Free core**: Full rules engine with starter captains, crews, upgrades, and ships.
- **Unlocks**: Captains, crew packs, upgrade packs, and a founder bundle for complete roster access.

## Platform & Delivery
- **Web client**: Runs the shared rules engine for consistency across modes.
- **Cross-platform reach**: Designed for desktop and mobile browsers; tabletop rules remain the single source of truth.

## Audience
- Strategy players who want simultaneous planning without scheduling friction.
- Groups that enjoy deterministic outcomes and visible tradeoffs.
- Solo captains who want immediate progression and clear post-turn information.
