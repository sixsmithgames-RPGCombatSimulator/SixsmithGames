## Overview
MasterTyping turns typing practice into a light adventure. Players progress through lessons and challenges with feedback, while educators and parents can track progress without extra setup.

## Modes
- **Game mode**: Playful stages with themed prompts and light progression.
- **Pro mode**: Skill drills for speed and accuracy with stricter scoring.
- **Assessment mode**: Six-step assessment that pinpoints weaknesses (home row, weak fingers, shift keys, common patterns, speed) with upgradeable deep analytics for detailed breakdowns.
- **Exercise / specialized practice**: Targeted drills by skill focus to address weaknesses identified in assessments.

## Key Features
- **Progressive lessons**: Gradual difficulty, finger-position guidance, and accuracy feedback.
- **Challenge runs**: Timed or streak-based challenges to keep repetition engaging.
- **Assessment analytics**: Comprehensive six-exercise assessment with basic results for all users and optional deep analytics (per-exercise breakdowns, weakness detection, personalized recommendations).
- **Profiles and progress tracking**: Per-mode stats (game, pro, assessment, exercises), WPM progress chart, recent sessions, and persistent weakness tracking—all saved locally in the browser.
- **Adaptive pacing**: Adjusts difficulty based on performance to keep players in flow.
- **Kid-friendly themes**: Light narrative framing to feel like a game, not a worksheet.
- **Classroom-friendly**: Low friction for teachers; run assessments and review scores quickly.

## Monetization
- **Free**: Full access to core lessons, challenges, assessments (basic results), and core progress tracking.
- **Premium**: Unlock deep analytics on assessments (detailed breakdowns, weakness identification, personalized practice recommendations, character-level performance insights).

## Audience Notes
- Kids and families who want practice that feels like a game.
- Teachers who need quick assessments and progress visibility without setup overhead.
- Anyone building speed and accuracy with immediate feedback and low friction.
